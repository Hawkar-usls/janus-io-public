This repository update is a public research evidence snapshot.

It does not claim that Janus has proven an advantage, profit, or superiority over the mirror/control path.

- Janus has accumulated accepted-proof telemetry.
- Janus has strict 50/50 accounting machinery.
- Janus has observer-only HRain/A18/Buzz swarm telemetry.
- Strict normalized windows remain mixed and must remain the arbiter.

Not allowed wording:

- Janus proves mining advantage.
- Janus is profitable.
- Janus is superior to the mirror.
- Swarm/bio/Tranception output changes PoW validity.

Safety boundary:

- WIRE/HASH/SUBMIT frozen.
- Mirror untouched.
- Observer-only layers do not control miner submit paths.

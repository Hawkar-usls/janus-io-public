## Current next question

The next narrow engineering question is:

> At small target values, how many batches are already admitted, dispatched,
> executing, completed, or buffered when the target gate first becomes
> authoritative?

A useful diagnostic sweep should use fresh processes, isolated outputs, complete
shutdown between runs, and targets covering zero, one, primes, composites, and
queue/chunk residues. It should compare post-target batches with equal-size
pre-target and mid-window controls.

Any finding about prime/composite targets remains exploratory until queue depth,
worker count, dispatch chunk size, and modulo effects are separated.

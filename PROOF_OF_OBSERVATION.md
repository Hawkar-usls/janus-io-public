# JANUS Proof-of-Observation

This repository records the first canonical public formulation of
**Proof-of-Observation inside the JANUS research line**, dated **2026-07-10**.

Read the full specification and A18 implementation lineage:

- [Canonical Proof-of-Observation record](docs/proof-of-observation.md)
- [Machine-readable origin record](docs/proof-of-observation-origin-record.json)

Canonical principle:

```text
Do not trust an impression.
Trust the chain of observations.
```

Proof-of-Observation separates facts, deterministic derived metrics, and claims;
links observer records for integrity; preserves provenance and uncertainty; and
fails closed when exact exposure, comparability, or independent holdout evidence
is unavailable.

It is not a claim of machine consciousness, distributed consensus, SHA-256
prediction, nonce prediction, mining advantage, or profitability.
